Hospitals of the University of Pennsylvania-Penn Presbyterian is a 784-bed general medical and surgical facility with 37,925 admissions in the most recent year reported. It performed 14,972 annual inpatient and 10,608 outpatient surgeries. Its emergency room had 64,423 visits. Hospitals of the University of Pennsylvania-Penn Presbyterian is a teaching hospital. It is also accredited by the Commission on Accreditation of Rehabilitation Facilities (CARF).
